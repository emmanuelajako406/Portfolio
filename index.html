Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered here

How long does a website take to build?

This will depend on which plan you choose. It can be as fast as 1-2 days with the Basic plan, or up to 4 weeks with the Pro Plan. The speed of delivery will also depend on how quick you are with communication and feedback.

I have a design already. Can you build it?

Yes! If you have existing design files in formats like Figma or PSD, I can turn them into a functional website. Once the development is complete and meets your satisfaction, I'll deliver the finished website to you. ‍

How much do websites cost?

Websites can vary in price based on a multitude of factors.

However, with our proprietary plug-and-play system, we can create a high-performing sales website for your pest company for a fraction of the cost of our competitors.

With all that said, our average site price is $1,500 with no additional hidden fees.
‍

What if I don’t like the design?

I offer a 100% money-back guarantee up until you see the first draft of the design, so if you don't like it at that point, I'll refund your deposit. Our #1 goal is to create a website that attracts new customers to your business, but we also want to ensure that you LOVE your site. For this reason, we have a revision process allowing you to provide feedback to ensure you are happy with the final result. Each site comes with a 100% satisfaction guarantee—if you don't like the design, we will keep working on it until you do. ‍

How does Payment Work?

A 50% payment is required upfront before the project begins. Another 25% is due upon finalizing the designs, and the remaining 25% is payable once development is complete and you have approved the site.

I'm kind of skeptical about this, what should I do?

If you're feeling skeptical, I completely understand. It's essential to feel confident in your decision. I recommend scheduling a consultation with me to discuss any concerns you may have.
