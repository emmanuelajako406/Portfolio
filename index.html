Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered here

How long does a website take to build?

This will depend on which plan you choose. It can be as fast as 2-4 days for a Basic website, or up to 4 weeks a more Complex Site. The speed of delivery will also depend on how quick you are with communication and feedback.

I have a design already. Can you build it?

Yes! If you have existing design files in formats like Figma or PSD, I can turn them into a functional website. Once the development is complete and meets your satisfaction, I'll deliver the finished website to you. ‍

How does Payment Work?

A 70% payment is required upfront before the project begins. and the remaining 30% is payable once development is complete and you have approved the site.
